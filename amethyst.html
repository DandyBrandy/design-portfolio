Print Ad

Background

Design a print ad for a mother's day special at a seaside resort. The logo was provided by the client as well as a style guide with appropriate colors and fonts.

Outcome

I researched competing resorts on the island to get an idea of how they were advertising their specials. The higher end places used full bleed photographs with minimal text/graphics which is why I chose to also go this route. A gorgeous photo of the beach is perfect to put white over and showcases the reason for coming to the resort. The logo and main headline are large and at the top, the details are down at the bottom on the sand. The location details are separated by diamonds to give separation and make it easier to scan.
