Package and Logo Design

Background

Create a logo and package design for a natural skincare company that will reflect both luxury and care for the consumer.

Outcome

The logo is a simple leaf illustration that has multiple version suitable for different backgrounds and applications. The color scheme is gold and two shades of green to mimic nature and luxury. The font selected is IvyMode which pairs nicely with the thin lines of the logo. The packaging itself will incorporate faux wood lids, green containers boxes made from recycled paper. If desired the client could have a metallic finish to the gold. The different styles of the logo can be used as an identifier for different products. The gold, solid leaf can be used on eyecare products, the gold, outline leaf on face moisturizers and the solid green leaf for body products.
