Magazine Layout

Background

Create a magazine layout for a lifestyle magazine geared towards millennials needing help with plant care. The border design is based on the shape of plant terrariums that were popular for housing succulents. Icons were custom created for the reader to easily find information on plant care. Dark and light greens were combined with a neutral, ballerina pink and white to capture a minimal style.

Outcome

The border design is based on the shape of plant terrariums that were popular for housing succulents. Icons were custom created for the reader to easily find information on plant care. Dark and light greens were combined with a neutral, ballerina pink and white to capture both a minimal and natural style.
