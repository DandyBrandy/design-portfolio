Logo Design

Background

Create a logo for a children's scuba school to place on various marketing materials such as business cards, website, stationery and brochures.

Outcome

I wanted to create a logo for this brand that appealed to both parents and children alike. Parents will want safety to be the focus on choosing a scuba school and kids will want to focus on fun. I created a simple fish logo with a larger fish guiding a smaller fish to show the teacher/student relationship. The text is slightly rounded to conform to the shape of the fish without being difficult to read. I chose to use different shades of blue as it's a trustworthy color and symbolizes water. Competitors to this brand used a lot of loud colors in their branding and complicated illustrations, but I feel that by focusing on a simpler form and bright but more professional color scheme will give parents the confidence they need when selecting the scuba school for their kids.
